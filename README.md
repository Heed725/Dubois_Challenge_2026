# #DuBoisChallenge2026

My entries for the **2026 W.E.B. Du Bois Data Visualization Challenge**, organized by [Anthony Starks](https://github.com/ajstarks/dubois-data-portraits/tree/master/challenge/2026).

## About the Challenge

The Du Bois Challenge is an annual celebration of the data visualization legacy of [W.E.B. Du Bois](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W._E._B._Du_Bois), who produced a groundbreaking series of hand-drawn charts, maps, and graphs for the **1900 Paris Exposition** documenting the conditions of Black Americans after emancipation. The goal is to recreate these iconic visualizations using modern tools.
